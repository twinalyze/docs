Want deeper insights into your ad monetization?

The **Ad Analytics SDK** helps you monitor and analyze ad behavior across major networks like **AdMob**, **Facebook**, and **AdX**   so you can optimize revenue, reduce errors, and make data-driven ad decisions.

---

## What It Tracks

- Ad Requests & Loads
- Ad Shows & Clicks
- Ad Failures (with error details)
- Revenue Data (micros, currency, and precision for AdMob)

Whether you're using **Interstitial**, **Banner**, or **App Open Ads**, this SDK logs everything that matters *at the right moment*.
